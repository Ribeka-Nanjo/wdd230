Site/Domain Name

Kotoku Chamber of Commerce

The site's purpose

This site aims to help support local business owners and entrepreneurs who promote the interests of their local businesses community in Kotoku, Japan. It also offers opportunities to share valuable resources, new networking, and educational opportunities to satisfy their business needs and improve their business qualities.
